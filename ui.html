Instructions:

Please fill out the form with the required information for TABIL 2.0 setup. Select your desired Station ID, Buffer Time, Arrival Countdown, and Platform Countdown values. You can also specify two separate time ranges for both weekdays and weekends. Check the "Verify revenue train IDs" checkbox if you want to enable revenue train ID verification. Click the "Generate" button to generate the setup file.
